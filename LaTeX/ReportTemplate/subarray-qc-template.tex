	\subsection{Aptamer ANOVA Plots}
		\subsubsection{Raw Data}
      These plots diplay the contribution of variance from three sources,
      inter-slide variation, inter-subarray variation, and inter-spot
      variation. 
      The aptamers are sorted by the sum of inter-slide and inter-subarray CV,
      with the contribution from inter-sport variation plotted on top.
      This causes some of the rows of aptamer CVs to have y-axes with different
      ranges, however has the advantage of ordering the aptamers by the CV of
      their observed RFU value.
      The motivation for this is aptamers with a high inter-spot CV can still
      be very consistent across subarrays since there are 10 replicate spots on
      each subarray.\\
